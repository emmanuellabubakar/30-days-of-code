Top Favourite Tv Shows

In Time

A 2011 American science fiction action film that stars Amanda Seyfried and Justin Timberlake as inhabitants in a society where people stop aging at 25. Instead of using paper money, a new economic system uses time as currency, and each person has a clock on their arm that counts down how long they have to live.

Miraculous Ladybug

The series features two Parisian teenagers, Marinette Dupain-Cheng and Adrien Agreste, who transform into the superheroes Ladybug and Cat Noir, respectively, to protect the city from supervillains.

I am not a Robot

Yoo Seung-ho as Kim Min-kyu lives a luxurious but isolated life due to a severe allergy to physical contact with other human beings. Through his company, he owns Aji 3, an android robot which gets sent to him to be tested. When the robot is damaged in an accident, Ji-ah, the robot's model is used to replace Aji 3. He develops feelings for Ji-ah as the robot and falls in love with her.
